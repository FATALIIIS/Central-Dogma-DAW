Hi, my name is John, I'm 23 years old, and my goal with this project is simple: to learn programming with something I'm very passionate about, which is electronic music.

DAW Central Dogma is a Dogma Central, where you can find information and good examples of each of the electronic music genres we currently have.

Because electronic music is extremely volatile, it's very important that you know that many songs presented here may belong to at least two genres, or even more.

That said, feel free to browse the menus and find your newest favorite electronic music genre.
